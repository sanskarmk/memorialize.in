From Story to Sculpture

Three steps. One extraordinary gift.

Describe the Person

Upload a photo, enter a name, profession, hobbies, personality — the more you share, the more personal the design. Our AI reads between the lines.

AI Generates Concepts

Our generative model produces 4 unique 3D design concepts tailored to the person's identity. You pick your favourite — or ask for a fresh batch.

03

We Print & Ship

Your chosen design is converted to STL, printed on our precision 3D setup, quality-checked, and shipped to your door in a premium gift box.
